About RBO

Built in a Texas Garage

Red Beard Ops started as a one-man knifemaking operation — raw steel, hand tools, and a garage full of sawdust and scale. What began as a passion project turned into a full YouTube channel documenting every build, every mistake, and every lesson learned along the way.

If it involves a forge, an angle grinder, or a belt sander — it probably ends up on camera.
